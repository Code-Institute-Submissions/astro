Constellations

Constellations are patterns formed by stars as percieved from Earth. The patterns usually resemble animals, mythical creatures or innanimate objects and on this page you'll find 12 constellations that 12 zodiac signs were named after. You can learn more about each using the interactive 3D models on this page, they are draggable, rotateable and clickable.
